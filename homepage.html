Types of Victoria’s Secret Perfume!

Bare Vanilla

Bare Vanilla offers a warm and subtly sweet vanilla scent with hints of cashmere and whipped vanilla. It’s perfect for fans of cozy, slightly sensual fragrances that linger on the skin for hours.

Pure Seduction

Pure Seduction combines red plum and freesia, creating a flirty, fruity aroma. This youthful scent is ideal for casual daily wear, especially during spring and summer.

Love Spell

Love Spell is a sweet, fruity scent with notes of cherry blossom and peach. This classic fragrance is known for its romantic, uplifting vibe and is great for day-to-day use.
